Everything in one app

Built for South African learners

Stop hopping between university websites, bursary PDFs and APS calculators. Scancourse pulls it all into one place.

📄

Scan your report card

Take a photo or upload a PDF — we'll read your marks and calculate your APS automatically. Add multiple reports (Grade 11, NSC, supplementary) and we keep your best mark per subject.

🎓

Find courses you qualify for

1,600+ programmes across all 25 SA public universities, filtered by your APS, subjects and preferred field. See exactly which subjects you're missing or how many APS points you're short.

💰

Discover bursaries

110+ NSFAS, government, corporate and foundation bursaries — matched to your marks, province and dream career. Never miss a deadline again.

🏠

Accommodation

NSFAS-accredited residences near every university, with prices and capacity. Plan where you'll live before you accept an offer.

📅

Track your applications

Save courses and bursaries you're applying to. We remind you of deadlines so nothing slips through the cracks.

✨

AI assistant

Stuck writing a motivation letter? Need to understand a university's requirements? Our AI helper is built specifically for SA learners.
